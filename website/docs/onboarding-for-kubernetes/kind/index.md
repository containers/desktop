# Onboarding Kubernetes on your workstation with Kind and Podman

Podman Desktop can help you run [Kind-powered](https://kind.sigs.k8s.io/) local Kubernetes clusters on a container engine, such as Podman.

#### Procedure

1. [Install the `kind` CLI](/docs/onboarding-for-kubernetes/kind/installing-kind).
2. [On Windows, configure Podman in rootful mode](/docs/onboarding-for-kubernetes/kind/configuring-podman-for-kind-on-windows).
3. [Create a Kind cluster](/docs/onboarding-for-kubernetes/kind/creating-a-kind-cluster).

#### Next steps

1. [Work with your Kind cluster](/docs/kubernetes/kind).
1. [Restart your Kind cluster](/docs/onboarding-for-kubernetes/kind/restarting-your-kind-cluster).
1. [Delete your Kind cluster](/docs/onboarding-for-kubernetes/kind/deleting-your-kind-cluster).
